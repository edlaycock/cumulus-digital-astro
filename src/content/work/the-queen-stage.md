The Queen Stage is an independent pub in the heart of Effingham Village, at the gateway to the Surrey Hills, fully renovated in 2022 around the world of cycling and locally sourced produce. We built a cycling-themed website with Bronze and Anthracite as the primary colours — matching the interior and exterior of the pub — and produced logo animations and creative to carry the same theme through their social media.
