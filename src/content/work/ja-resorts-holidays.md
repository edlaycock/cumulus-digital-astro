challenge: JA Resorts & Hotels operates award-winning beach and mountain properties across the UAE and Indian Ocean, so its holidays brand had to convey five-star escapism the moment a visitor lands. The site needed to sell the feeling of a luxury break — sun, sea and space — while making it easy to move from inspiration to booking.
approach: Cumulus led with full-bleed resort photography — infinity pools, palms and beachfront — so the destinations carry the page. Cool blues and warm stone tones drawn from the pool-and-sand imagery keep the palette calm and premium, with the JA wordmark kept understated over the visuals so nothing competes with the setting.
result: A polished, image-first experience that matches the upmarket positioning of the resorts and invites travellers to picture their stay.
