challenge: Ras Al Khaimah is the UAE's adventure emirate — authentic Arabian getaways, desert scenery and the world's longest zip line at Jebel Jais, within easy reach of Dubai. Ras Al Khaimah Holidays needed a destination-dedicated booking site that could sell the emirate's story and its luxury resorts — from the Ritz-Carlton and Waldorf Astoria to Rixos and Anantara — with live offers, package pricing and clear routes to book.
approach: This is exactly the kind of project our 25+ years in the travel industry were made for. We designed and built a destination-first site that leads with the emirate's beaches, mountains and adventure, backed by a full roster of resort pages, special offers with per-person package pricing, holiday types and prominent phone and booking journeys — the structure a tour operator needs to convert lookers into bookers.
result: A polished, conversion-focused destination site that presents Ras Al Khaimah's luxury resorts and adventure credentials to UK travellers, with live offers and clear booking paths throughout.
