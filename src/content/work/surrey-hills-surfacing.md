challenge: Surrey Hills Surfacing delivers tarmac driveways, paths, repairs and commercial surfacing — from car parks to school playgrounds and line marking — across Cobham, Weybridge, Esher and the wider Surrey Hills. A trades business that wins work through local search and word of mouth needed a professional web presence that matched the quality of their work on the ground and made it effortless to request a quote.
approach: We designed and built a bespoke, mobile-first website structured around the two things that matter for a local contractor — showcasing completed residential and commercial projects, and making contact frictionless from any device. The site presents their full service range, from tarmac driveways and resin surfacing to schools and line marking, alongside the credentials that build trust, including their Marshalls-accredited installer status. Cumulus continues to host and maintain the site.
result: A fast, professional site on the company's own domain that puts their finished work and credentials in front of local customers searching for surfacing across Surrey.
