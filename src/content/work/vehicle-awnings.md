challenge: Vehicle Awnings is a division of MBO-Power supplying awnings for vans, campers and motorhomes — a specialist, fitment-specific product range where buyers need to be confident they are choosing the right awning for their vehicle. The business needed an online store that made a technical, niche product easy to browse, understand and buy.
approach: What started off being a very simple website showing the products on offer, soon turned into something a little more complex when vehicle awnings brought the Thule brand into the business. The owners at the start did not want a full e-commerce website and so each product call to action was an enquiry form.
result: The store gives Vehicle Awnings a dedicated, easy-to-navigate sales channel for a specialist product, helping customers find and buy the right awning for their vehicle online.
