challenge: Fidelity is an ICAEW-chartered accountancy practice in Hersham offering tax, accounting and assurance for local businesses. Their positioning — local, personal business support — needed a website that felt as trustworthy and approachable as the service itself, and made it easy to get straight through to a real person.
approach: We designed and built a clean, confident site around Fidelity's promise of straightforward advice, practical solutions and personal service. The design leads with a clear, benefit-led message, the ICAEW Chartered Accountants credential, and a personal call to action — "Speak to Richard" — rather than an anonymous contact form, with the practice's fresh blue-and-charcoal identity carried throughout.
result: The new site is in final testing ahead of its public launch at fidelityaccountants.com — check back soon for the live link.
