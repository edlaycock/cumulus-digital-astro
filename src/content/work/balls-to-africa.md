approach: The website build was an enjoyable one knowing that we were just part of something that will be making a difference to children that are missing out on basic sporting goods that is so fundamental in the physical and mental health. result: The charity now has an approachable online home that tells its story through real imagery and makes its collect-and-distribute model easy to understand.
