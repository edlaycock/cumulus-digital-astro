OakBridge Group is a groundworks and foundations contractor working in and around Cobham, Leatherhead and Surrey, handling everything from small residential installations to large commercial and educational projects. We took over the OakBridge website and changed its architecture to allow more fluid changes to the theme, with better use of images and closer integration with the logo and brand — and built a new area where the team can add and manage their previous projects themselves.
