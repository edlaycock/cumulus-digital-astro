challenge: The Mews is a small, high-end development of four bespoke contemporary mews houses in a gated community within the Effingham Conservation Area, on the edge of the Surrey Hills. A boutique scheme like this needed a marketing site that felt considered and premium rather than a generic estate-agent listing, presenting the homes to prospective buyers with the right sense of quality and place.
approach: The Mews Effingham is within the picturesque Effingham Conservation Area, the mews is a short stroll from the village shops providing everyday essentials. Ranmore Common and the Surrey Hills, an area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, provides some of the best walking, cycling and horse riding countryside in the south of England and Effingham Golf Club is also nearby. Effingham Junction (2 miles) provides regular rail services to London and the A3 gives easy access to central London and the M25 (junction 10). The mews is within the catchment areas for both Howard of Effingham and St Lawrence’s schools.
result: The one-page site gives the development a refined, self-contained shop window that matches the quality of the homes and makes it easy for prospective buyers to register interest.
