The brand's traditional, personal feel needed to carry onto the web without looking dated, and customers had to be able to browse and enquire with ease.
approach: We kept the traditional, upmarket feel of the Jeremy Hobbs brand while adding modern usability and future-proofing the build. Since launch we also look after their weekly newsletters on an ongoing basis.
result: A refined, easy-to-use site that reflects the shop's premium, independent character and guides visitors towards visiting or hiring.
