challenge: Sussex Groundworks is a Lancing-based contractor handling demolition, land clearance, footings and substructures for homeowners, developers and commercial clients. As a hands-on trade business, they needed a website that conveyed capability and scale without the polish of a corporate build, and gave prospective clients a clear picture of the services they cover across the region.
approach: We built a clear, services-led website covering the full range of Sussex Groundworks' work — demolition, land clearance, footings and substructure — for their homeowner, property-developer and commercial clients across Sussex, with the brand's cerulean and charcoal palette carried throughout.
result: The site gives Sussex Groundworks a credible, professional presence that reflects the scale of work they take on and makes it easy for local clients to reach them.
