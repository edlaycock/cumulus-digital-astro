challenge: Abbey Group Services is a contractor working across construction and renewable-energy disciplines, including solar installations, for commercial, industrial and residential clients. A firm covering such a broad range of work needed a website that could convey scale and ambition while making its full spread of services easy to navigate.
approach: The website build was an amalgamation of the groups 3 divisions and bringing these all into one cohesive website. It has been designed and developed to showcase the Abbey Group Services strengths and accurately reflect their brand positioning – presenting the group as the professional and highly experienced specialising in Electrical, Fire & Security, and Renewables. The website boasts a clean and consistent navigation to help you find your way around, and is also fully responsive across all devices and web browsers.
result: The result is a professional, ambitious web presence that reflects the breadth of the contractor's capabilities and positions the firm credibly across its markets.
