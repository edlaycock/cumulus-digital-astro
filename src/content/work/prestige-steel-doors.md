challenge: DG Installations needed a distinct identity for a new division focused on internal steel-and-glass doors, a premium product for luxury homes and commercial spaces. The brand had to feel elevated and design-led from launch, separate from the parent company yet credible on quality.
approach: The main focus around the website design was to follow the theme of the logo and brand highlights. The Bronze and Anthracite primary colours are shown from start to finish. The Font types show strong headers and body text that also is seen on the physical literature and signage. Creatives and logo animations were created by Cumulus Digital to accompany their Social Media activity.
result: Prestige Steel Doors launched with a cohesive, premium identity and site that positions it clearly at the luxury end of the market.
