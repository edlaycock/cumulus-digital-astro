A website is not a fire-and-forget purchase. Our maintenance plans cover everything needed to keep your site fast, secure and current: third-party software patched and up to date, page images compressed, scripts loading optimally, and regular checks to make sure the website hasn't been compromised.

Plans range from bare-bones cover for simple sites to comprehensive monthly care that includes image and text changes and new pages as your business grows. Every plan is tailored to your website's individual architecture — talk to us about what yours needs.
